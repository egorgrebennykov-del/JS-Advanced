You will be given an array of strings representing a list of restaurants with their workers.

When the [Send] button is clicked:

* Display the best restaurant among all added restaurants together with its average salary and best salary.
* If a restaurant appears more than once in the input array, add the new workers to the existing ones and update the average salary and best salary values.
* The best restaurant is the one with the highest average salary. If two restaurants have the same average salary, the best restaurant is the one that was added first.
* Display all workers in the best restaurant together with their salaries.
* The workers in the best restaurant must be sorted by their salaries in descending order.

Input

The input will be received from the given textarea in the form of an array of strings. Each string represents a restaurant with its workers.

Example input:

["Mikes - Steve 1000, Ivan 200, Paul 800", "Fleet - Maria 850, Janet 650"]

Output

The output contains two strings:

The first one is the best restaurant in the format:

Name: {restaurant name} Average Salary: {restaurant avgSalary} Best Salary: {restaurant bestSalary}

The avgSalary and bestSalary must be formatted to the second decimal point.

The second one is all the workers in that restaurant in the following format:

Name: {worker name} With Salary: {worker salary} Name: {worker2 name} With Salary: {worker2 salary} Name: {worker3 name} With Salary: {worker3 salary} â€¦
